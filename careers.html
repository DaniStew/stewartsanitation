Join Our Team!

Proudly owned and operated since 1994, Stewart Sanitation is looking for a residential/commercial route driver to add to our team. If you love the outdoors and would love to work Monday through Friday and be home every night this may be the job for you! The candidate must be very reliable, self-motivated, and have a great attitude. The candidate must be able to work independently, and work well with others, and possess good communication skills. A commercial driver's license is preferred but we may consider training the right person. The candidate must have a good driving record. Mechanical aptitude a plus! Pay is based on experience, dependability, and performance.
